Privacy Policy for QuoteFlow: Daily Scroll Quotes

Effective date: February 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QuoteFlow: Daily Scroll Quotes ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the QuoteFlow: Daily Scroll Quotes mobile app (the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect limited information needed to operate the App:

  • Anonymous account identifier generated through anonymous authentication.
  • App activity data (for example: view, save, unsave, share events) with timestamps and context.
  • Saved quotes and preference data (such as reminder time and settings).
  • Technical service metadata processed by providers (for example: IP address and request logs).

We do not ask for your name, phone number, or email address in the App.

How We Use Information

  • Provide app features such as daily quotes, saved quotes, and reminders.
  • Sync your saved content for your anonymous app account.
  • Improve reliability, understand usage, and prevent abuse.
  • Maintain security and troubleshoot issues.

Notifications

If enabled, reminders are scheduled on your device. Notification preferences are stored locally, and may be synced with your app profile settings.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the App, such as backend and infrastructure providers (for example, Supabase), under contractual safeguards.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for operational, legal, and security purposes. Data stored on your device can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the App.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

QuoteFlow: Daily Scroll Quotes may be used by children under 13. We limit data collection and do not require direct contact details (like name or email) for core app use.

Parents or guardians may contact us to request review or deletion of data associated with a child's app use.

Your Choices

  • Disable notifications in app settings or device settings.
  • Remove local data by clearing app data or uninstalling the App.
  • Request deletion help by contacting us.

International Transfers

Information may be processed in countries other than your own based on provider operations.
